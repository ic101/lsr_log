Data logger for a one cylinder two stroke land speed race motorcycle 

Circuit boards:
Teensy 4.1 - Core processor board
EGT breakout board - https://www.adafruit.com/product/1778
GPS Module - GPS NEO-6M
7 Segs - 8 Digit MAX7219 (SPI input)



System design is based on Teensy 4.1 to log and display:
RPM - Rotations per minute of the crankshaft
EGT - Exhaust gas temperature
MPH - Speed of the device


Other I/O
LEDs - Shift and other alarms
Switches - four inputs 2 buttons 2 switches?

Debug stuff
Uart? Serial Monitor?
